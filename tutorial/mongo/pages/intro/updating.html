Updating 
db.users.updateOne({age: 26}, {$set: {age: 27}})
Same as 
db.users.updateOne({age: 26}, {$inc: {age: 3}})
To increment
db.users.updateOne({age: 26}, {$rename: {name: "firstName"}})
db.users.updateOne({age: 26}, {$unset: {age: ""}})
This will remove the age
db.users.updateOne({age: 26}, {$push: {hobbies: "Footballing"}})
db.users.updateOne({age: 26}, {$pull: {hobbies: "Bowling"}})

db.users.updateMany({address: {$exists: true}}, {$unset: {address: ""})

db.users.replaceOne({age: 30}, {name: "John"})

The above will be used when we want to update the first item in the collection
